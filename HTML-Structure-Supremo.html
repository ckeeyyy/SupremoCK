Living Simple

by: Aarushi Tewari
August 24, 2023

All you need is love. And simplicity. Must have must simplicity.

At its root, simple living is cutting to the fundamentals: a life driven by what’s most important and valuable to you. In that sense, material items don’t come to the forefront of the discussion. To live simply, we must first focus on our values and the aspects of our life that are vital to our existence. In my opinion, living a simple life means living a life most fulfilling to you because you are investing your time and energy into things that matter the most. 

        6 Ways to Live a Simple Life
        1. Become aware of what’s most important to you
        2. Invest your time in meaningful things
        3. Give your energy to things that actually matter
        4. Differentiate facts from hypothesis
        5. Leave space to do nothing
        6. Practice gratitude for every little blessing
